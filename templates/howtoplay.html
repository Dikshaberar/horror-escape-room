📖 HOW TO PLAY

🎯 OBJECTIVE

You are trapped in a haunted building. Explore 4 terrifying rooms, collect clues, solve puzzles, and find the escape code before the 5-minute timer runs out!

🎮 HOW TO PLAY

  • Click action buttons to interact with objects
  • Items you collect appear in your inventory
  • Use items to unlock doors and solve puzzles
  • Move to the next room when you find required item

❤️ LIVES / SANITY

  • You start with 3 lives
  • Jump scares cost 1 life!
  • Wrong puzzle answers cost 1 life
  • Risky actions may cost 1 life
  • Lose all 3 = GAME OVER 💀

⏰ TIMER

You have exactly 5 minutes to escape. When it hits 0:00 it's over. More time remaining = higher score!

🔢 THE CODE

Find 4 code pieces hidden across the rooms. Combine them to form the final 4-digit escape code. Look carefully at every clue!

😱 JUMP SCARES

Random jump scares can happen at ANY moment. They get more frequent the deeper you go. Turn volume up for full experience... if you dare! 👻
